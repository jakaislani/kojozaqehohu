    The Zeus Network unveiled a brand-new original series in 2022, Baddies South. This eagerly awaited show, a spin-off from Bad Girls Club, is causing a buzz. Fans are eager to learn when the Baddies South episodes air and when fresh drama hits the screen. With familiar faces like executive producer Natalie Nunn, the series takes the cast on a thrilling journey across the southern USA. Destinations include bustling cities like Atlanta, Charlotte, Nashville, New Orleans, and Houston. Judging by the fiery trailer, viewers can expect heaps of drama and excitement.

    Baddies South: A Successor to Baddies ATL

    Following the triumph of the Baddies ATL spin-off in 2021, Baddies South emerges as the latest addition in 2022. The original Bad Girls Club, which first graced screens in 2006 on Oxygen, held the audience captive until 2017. Following this legacy, Baddies ATL aired from February to March in 2021. Now, Baddies South has taken the stage, kicking off its run on June 12th, 2022.

    Mark Your Calendar: Air Time for Baddies South

    Baddies South airs on The Zeus Network every Sunday at 8 pm ET. Episode 10, titled “Girls Gone Wild,” made waves on Sunday, August 14th, offering viewers a wild ride through New Orleans. The diverse cast, featuring Rollie, Slim, Chrisean Rock, Jela, Scotty, Anne Moore, Persuasian, Natalie Nunn, and Bri, promises entertainment galore.

    Catch the Action: New Episodes Await

    For ardent fans of Baddies South, tuning in is a breeze with a subscription to the Zeus Network. Subscriptions are available at $5.99 per month or a discounted annual rate of $59.99. Additionally, episodes are conveniently accessible on-demand via the Zeus Network website. As of the latest update, 10 gripping episodes of Baddies South have hit the screens, and enthusiasts can look forward to fresh content every Sunday.

    This ensures a weekly dose of riveting drama, setting the stage for a memorable viewing experience. With a cast that knows how to keep the audience hooked, Baddies South is here to make Sunday nights sizzle.
