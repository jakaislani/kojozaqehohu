    Chad Gable Pens Heartfelt Tribute To Kurt Angle

    In a heartfelt post on Instagram today, Chad Gable wrote about what last night’s match with Kurt Angle meant to him, with a picture of a signed VHS copy of Angle’s “It’s True, It’s True” home video release accompanying his words.

    Gable writes:

    “What’s that saying about heroes becoming rivals?⁣
    @therealkurtangle signed this for me at Mall of America in 2000. Wished me luck after I told him I was going to make the Greco-Roman Olympic Team and then wrestle in WWE. ⁣
    20 years later, here we are…⁣
    Life is great, but sometimes, it can be really special. I’m grateful for last night. Thank you Kurt.”
