    Gussy Lau is well-known as the boyfriend of Angela Aguilar.

    Gussy Lau is a Mexican music composer, singer, songwriter and entrepreneur.

    He was born Luis Abraham Buelna Vea on June 6, 1988 in Mocorito, Sinaloa, Mexico.

    Gussy Lau has written and composed several songs, including Vivir la Vida, En Contra de Mi Voluntad, Hombre de Trabajo, Con Toda la Fuerza, Los Gustos Que Me Doy, Desde Que La Vi, Ni Diablo Ni Santo, Comere Callado, Vol. 2, Calidad y Cantidad, Ni Juez Ni Parte Christian: Nace un borracho, Ya No Soy El Mismo, They didn’t tell you wrong, et al.

    The singer has worked with popular musicians such as Christian Nodal, Los Rojos, Banda Los Recoditos, Calibre 50, Hijos De Barron, and among others.

    In March 2021, Gussy Lau was signed to Equinoccio Records, which is run by Pepe Aguilar, his girlfriend’s father. Gussy owns a music studio in Mexico.

    In April 2022, one of his close friends leaked photos of him and Angela Aguilar kissing on social media. He later confirmed that they were dating. The two started dating in mid-February 2022.

    Gussy Lau won an X2 Latin Grammy award, and on September 2022, he was nominated for the fourth time to the Latin Grammys.
