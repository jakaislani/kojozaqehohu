    ➡ Alaia Furniturewalla Biography

    Aalia Furniturewalla is a renowned, emerging face of the Indian film industry as she gave stellar performances in the movies and got acclaimed. The young, gorgeous actress is also known by her stage name Alaya Furniturewalla. She is the daughter of Pooja Bedi and Farhan Furniturewalla and works in Bollywood films. The actress belongs to the actors’ family as her grandfather Kabir Bedi is also a great actor, and her grandmother Protime Bedi was a dancer. She made her film debut through a Bollywood comedy film named ‘Jawani Janeman’ in which she appeared as a lead actress. While the film was released in the year 2020. Well, we have some unknown facts through which you will know more about her.

    8 Things You Didn’t Know About Alaia Furniturewalla

    1- Appealing Looks

    The emerging actress Alaia Furniturewalla is not famous for just her acting skills, yet she is known for her appealing looks that set her apart. Her light brown-colored eyes and her black hair always give her fascinating looks that win the hearts of many.

    2- Religion/Nationality

    The actress is basically from India as she has Indian nationality. While she follows Zoroastrian Parsi religion. Alaya attended Jamnabai Narsee School, located in Mumbai, India. Later, she joined New York University, but she dropped out and joined New York Film Academy. She was born into a family of talented actors and actresses.

    3- Food Habits

    The pretty gorgeous actress is a non-vegetarian and she loves eating seafood including fish, prawns, and a variety of food she loves the most.

    4- Love for Dancing & Doodling

    Despite having a busy schedule, the actress takes time out for her hobbies as she loves dancing and doodling that she always does in her leisure time.

    5- Gold Medalist

    Alaya Furniturewalla is a gold medalist and was honored with the Balakratna National Art Award in 2013, Gold Medal for performing excellently in the field of Art.

    6- She & her Mother

    The actress along with her mother Pooja Bedi got featured on the front page of India’s Oldest Women’s English Magazine ‘Femina’ in 2016.

    7- Attended Acting Workshops

    The stunning actress also attended several acting workshops to hone her acting skills along with Ritesh Kant and Abhishek Pandey. Alongside this, she is a well-trained Kathak and Contemporary dancer.

    She rose to fame after appearing in the Ponds Advertisement before shooting for the Bollywood film named ‘Jawani Jaaneman’ which was released in 2020.
